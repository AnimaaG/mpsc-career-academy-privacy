Account Deletion Request

MPSC Career Academy users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email from your registered email address
  2. Email subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Send to: mes.info.help@gmail.com , aniketghadi010@gmail.com

Data that will be deleted

  • User profile (name, email)
  • Authentication account
  • Purchased material access

Data that may be retained

  • Transaction records (for legal/compliance purposes)

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 working days.
